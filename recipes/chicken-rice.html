Chicken and Rice

Description

This recipe is a classic when it comes to simplicity and bulking. You can never go wrong with good ol' chicken and rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Rice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sriracha sauce

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of 2 cups of water to boil
  2. Bake chicken in oven for 69 minutes
  3. Pour 1 cup of rice into pot and set to low and cover
  4. Once chicken is done, slice into big pieces
  5. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  6. Once rice is done, combine with chicken and top with Sriracha

Dumplings

Description

If you're lucky enough to have an amazing dumpling plug who only supplies the most gas dumplings, then you'll be in for a treat. This recipe features one of the most well-known Chinese delicacies that people love around the world.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Dumplings (from reputable plug)
  • Chinese black vinegar
  • Lao Gan Ma (if you don't know what this then fuck off)

Steps

  1. Bring water in a pot to boil
  2. Once water is boiling, throw dumplings into pot
  3. Stir dumplings occasionally until water is boiling
  4. Once water is boiling, set a timer for 3 minutes with lid off
  5. Drain dumplings and move to bowl
  6. Mix vinegar with Lao Gan Ma for dipping sauce
